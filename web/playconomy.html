Playconomy: Revolutionizing Gaming Rewards

Back to Dashboard

Playconomy introduces real-world value into gaming ecosystems by integrating Bitcoin and UTXOs. Players can earn, trade, and own in-game assets, with OUTixs ensuring secure tracking and provenance of every digital item.

Technology Behind Playconomy

  • Bitcoin: Powers the in-game economy, allowing players to earn and trade real-world assets.
  • OUTixs: Tracks ownership and provenance of in-game items off-chain, reducing costs and ensuring security.
  • Nostr: Enables seamless communication and collaboration among players in decentralized gaming environments.

Use Cases

  • Games where players earn Bitcoin as rewards for completing challenges.
  • In-game marketplaces for trading items with real-world value.
  • Decentralized multiplayer experiences with secure ownership of digital assets.
