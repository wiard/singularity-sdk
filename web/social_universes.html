Social Universes combines the power of Nostr and OUTixs to create decentralized social platforms where users maintain ownership of their data and interactions. This ensures privacy, transparency, and user empowerment.

Technology Behind Social Universes

  • Nostr: Provides decentralized event-based messaging to enable seamless social interactions.
  • OUTixs: Anchors important social milestones to Bitcoin, creating an immutable record of key moments.
  • Bitcoin: Acts as the trust layer, ensuring all interactions are transparent and tamper-proof.

Use Cases

  • Decentralized social networking platforms free from corporate control.
  • Personal messaging systems with end-to-end encryption and no intermediaries.
  • Event-based communities where activities and milestones are publicly verifiable.
